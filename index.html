Biography

This site is currently under construction.

I'm Jack, a Multiskilled Electrical & Electronic Engineering Student at The University of Nottingham, maker of things and adventurer of places.


While I am developing a specialism in EEE, my background started in broader Engineering with a emphasis on Mechanical & Manufacturing.

My interests vary, though currently include the Internet of Things, Automation, Electric Vehicles and developing hardware and software.


I intend for this website to be used as not only a portfolio, but a general purpose collection of projects, adventures and anything else that I feel deserves a home.
